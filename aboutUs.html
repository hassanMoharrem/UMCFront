Welcome
To United Muslims of Canberra (UMC)

Who We Are:

United Muslims of Canberra (UMC) is a vibrant and inclusive community organization dedicated to fostering unity, understanding, and growth among the Muslim youth of Canberra. We believe in the potential of our young members and are committed to providing them with the tools, resources, and opportunities they need to thrive in today's world.

Our Vision:

To be a beacon of hope, guidance, and empowerment for the Muslim youth of Canberra, helping them navigate the challenges of modern society while staying true to their faith and values.

Our Mission:

Empowerment: Equip our youth with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to become leaders in their communities and beyond.

Why Focus on Youth?

The youth are not just the future of our community; they are its heartbeat. In a rapidly changing world, it's crucial that our young members feel grounded in their identity and empowered to make positive contributions to society. By investing in their development, we are ensuring a brighter future for all.
